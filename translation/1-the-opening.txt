In the Name of God, The Merciful, The Gracious,
Praise be to God— the Lord of all worlds,
the Merciful, the Gracious,
and the King of Judgment Day.
It is You alone whom we serve, and it is from You alone we seek aid.
Guide us along the straight path—
the path of those whom You have blessed, not those who arouse Your Anger nor those who stray away.
